The Grimace Lab

Your tight jaw is your body's loud whisper for liberation.

Most wellness programs create a false divide between deep practice and daily business. Grimace Consciousness bridges that gap. It is a somatic intervention for high-achievers who refuse to let the friction of everyday execution degrade their physical bodies. By transforming how you hold pressure at your desk or during social interactions, it turns your physical presence into an asset for higher creative capacity and autonomy.

A three-second diagnostic: Drop your eyebrows. Unhinge your jaw. If that felt like a massive relief, your body is currently carrying the structural stress of your environment, throttling your strategic intuition.

The 4-Week Journey
Phase I

Notice and Choose

Begin identifying your unique structural patterns of somatic contraction. We consciously map out where you locate your grimace—whether it accumulates within tight jaws, a locked stomach, or heavy eyebrows.

Phase II

Watch the Impulse

Study the exact moments your body triggers its somatic contractions. By closely tracking the underlying impulse to grimace, you foster the capacity for conscious inhibition and somatic space.

Phase III

Go Sideways

Disrupt old defaults and step outside of inherited bodily conditioning. This phase focuses entirely on forging new creative expressions, new somatic grimaces, and deeply aligned habits.

Phase IV

Settle In

Consolidating your somatic expansion. Fully adopting grimace consciousness as a sustained lifestyle, anchoring your baseline in an un-contracted, liberated state of creative flow.
